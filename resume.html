Project: Route Clearance System for Emergency Vehicles Using GPS and RFID

An approach designed to propose and overcome the common limitation of modern road traffic lights which make an emergency vehicle to slow-speed/stop. We used communication devices GPS and RFID technologies that are low cost and the range can be extended up to 500 meters, which is the most useful asset of this project.

  • • Every emergency vehicle is fitted with a unique RFID tag (like most toll tags) and the receivers located 500 meters away from the actual traffic junction on every lane sends out an interrupt in a private channel. This interrupt is received by the junction and is set to issue a “Prepare to Stop (Yellow)” in all the lanes except the lane in which the emergency vehicle is approaching to turn it to Green
  • • There are various parameters worked out using GPS to confirm the location of the receiver which further extends to confirm the lane that needs to turn Green
